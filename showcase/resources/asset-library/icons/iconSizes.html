Icon Sizes

There are three available sizes for the icons, these are small (10px), medium (16px), and large (28px). Medium is the default size, and the modifiers ebIcon_small and ebIcon_large respectively can be used for small and large icons respectively.


HTML

<i class="ebIcon ebIcon_small ebIcon_error"></i>
<i class="ebIcon ebIcon_error"></i>
<i class="ebIcon ebIcon_large ebIcon_error"></i>
