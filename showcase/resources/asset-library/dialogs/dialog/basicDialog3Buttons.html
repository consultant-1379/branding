Dialog With Three Buttons

The dialog box with three buttons is used when the user is required to make a choice and three conditions apply. It could be warning continued dialog box.
Primary text

Secondary text providing further details about possible options.

HTML

<div class="ebDialogBox">
  <div class="ebDialogBox-contentBlock">
    <div class="ebDialogBox-primaryText">Primary text</div>
    <div class="ebDialogBox-secondaryText ebText_alternative">
      <p>Secondary text providing further details about possible options.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="ebDialogBox-actionBlock">
    <button class="ebBtn ebBtn_color_darkBlue">Save</button>
    <button class="ebBtn">Discard</button>
    <button class="ebBtn">Cancel</button>
  </div>
</div>
